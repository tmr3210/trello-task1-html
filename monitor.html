MONITOR

A monitor is a piece of computer hardware that displays the
video and graphics information generated by a connected computer
through the computer's video card. Monitors are similar to TVs
but usually display information at a much higher resolution.
Also unlike televisions, monitors typically sit atop a desk
rather than being mounted on a wall. A monitor is sometimes
referred to as a screen, display, video display, video display
terminal, video display unit, or video screen.
